Corporate Profile

A Legacy of Engineering Excellence & Public Trust

Founded in 2013 by Mr. Y. Rama Krishna Reddy, YRK Constructions stands as a pillar of reliability in the Indian civil construction sector. Originating in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh, we have evolved into a Special Class contractor entrusted with large-scale irrigation systems, complex highway corridors, structural over-bridges, and expansive commercial yards across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and Odisha. Our footprint is defined by milestone achievements in regions including Hyderabad, Khammam, and Adilabad.

Government Procurement

Specialists in Central & State Govt. tenders, CPWD, MES, and PWD integrations.

Irrigation & Corridors

Executing critical water distribution networks, highway paving, and over-bridges.

Institutional Development

Constructing resilient academic complexes, healthcare facilities, and administrative hubs.
